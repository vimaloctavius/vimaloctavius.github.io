Abstract¶

Predict whether income exceeds $50K/yr based on census data. Also known as "Census Income" dataset.

Dataset from https://archive-beta.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/adult

Adult. (1996). UCI Machine Learning Repository.

We could aim to explore Classification algorithms: Xgboost, Support Vector Machines, Random Forest, Neural Network, Logistic Regression, K Nearest Neighbors
